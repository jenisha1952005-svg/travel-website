About Kanyakumari

Welcome to Kanyakumari, the southernmost tip of mainland India. Known historically as Cape Comorin, this district is a vibrant tapestry of majestic mountains, pristine beaches, and spiritual heritage. Whether you are here to witness the world-famous sunrise over the triple-sea confluence or to explore the legacy of Swami Vivekananda, our mission is to guide you through every hidden gem of this coastal paradise.

The Legend

Named after goddess kanya kumari,the town is steeped in ancient mythology and spiritual energy

The View

It is one of the few places on the earth where you can watch sunrise and sunset on the same beach

Landmarks

Home to the iconic 133ft Thiruvalluvar Statue and the serene Vivekanada Rock Memorial
